The Witcher

The Witcher is a series of fantasy action role-playing games
It is based on the book series of the same name by Polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski

The Witcher Game Series

The main series began in 2007 with the release of The Witcher, and concluded with 2015's The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. As of 2020, the series has three main standalone games, two expansion packs and three spin-off games.
